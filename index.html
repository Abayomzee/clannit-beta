Whats new

Improved Features

A Fresh, User-Friendly Forum Experience

Our new, modern forum interface makes it simple to post messages, join discussions, and interact with content. Whether you're sharing ideas, responding to threads, or liking posts, Clannit’s streamlined design keeps everything right at your fingertips. Experience community engagement like never before.

Seamless Guest Invites & Tracking

Easily invite guests to join your community with personalised invitations, and track their entry and exit with real-time insights. Whether you’re inviting one time guests or permanent guests our advanced guest tracking features give you full visibility and control, ensuring a secure and organised experience for all.

Manage Dues, Bills, and Payments with Ease

Clannit makes managing collections effortless. Keep track of estate dues, utility bills, and payment histories all in one place. Whether you're handling recurring payments or tracking past transactions, our intuitive system helps ensure everything is organised, up-to-date, and easy to access.

Whether you're hosting a small gathering or a large community event, our streamlined tools make it simple to coordinate every detail, track registrations, and ensure smooth entry for all attendees.

Marketplace

Whether you’re selling products, offering services, or trading items, our marketplace feature makes it easy to connect with buyers and sellers. Manage listings, browse offerings, and engage with trusted community members—all in one place. It’s the perfect platform to support local commerce and build stronger community connections.

Coming Soon

Clan Management

Clan management features to give you more control and flexibility, including role management for assigning specific responsibilities, the ability to appoint multiple estate administrators for shared oversight, and validation of domestic staff to ensure reliability and trust. These updates are designed to streamline Clan organization, enhance security, and improve efficiency, making it easier for you to manage and grow your Clan.
